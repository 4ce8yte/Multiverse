What's considered Media-Inspired OC?

A Media-Inspired OC is a character that borrows recognizable elements from existing media — like visual traits, personality quirks, themes, or vibes — without being a direct copy.

Think of it like this:

  • Inspired by: You grab a few ingredients from a character you like (hairstyle, color palette, attitude, weapon style), and mix them into something new that fits your world. You’re cooking your own dish — just with familiar spices.
  • Copied: You’re basically making a clone with a wig. Same outfit, same name, same backstory, just maybe species-swapped or with a color tweak. That crosses the line into plagiarism.

We can use queen Athena as an example, she's inspired on Caesar King from Zenless Zone Zero.

As you can see, Athena has similar traits like the hair, Body Type, Eyes and crown, but also multiple differences that tell them apart clearly and make the inspiration not as obvious, like the hair is slightly different and includes a color gradient, the crown is not Athena's antennae using the trait Beetle Horns, the eyes have a tone of red in them and she uses a lance instead of sword and shield.
